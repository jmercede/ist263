TOP COMIC OF THE WEEK

The Last Ronin

The last ronin takes place in a dystopian future of the TMNT universe. Only one of the turtles remains and New York has changed for the worse. The last turtle must find a way to live on and avenge his family's name as he faces the long-time criminal organization that took them away from him, they are known as The Foot!

Upcoming Comics

KING IN BLACK

As the year 2020 comes to a close, Marvel Comics will begin their first major comic event of 2021 starting in December. "KING IN BLACK" #1 will see Venom along with other heroes across the Marvel Universe battle the symbiote God KNULL. This will be an event like none other, taking horror to a whole new level in Marvel Comic history. As writer Donny Cates promises that this event will shake up the marvel universe forever. I've been a huge fan of Donny Cates run alongside the amazing artists who've been working on it so I'm definitely buying this and all the tie-ins!

WOLVERINE BLACK WHITE AND RED

"WOLVERINE BLACK, WHITE, & BLOOD" #1 starts to release beginning November 4 and will entail untold adventures of the mutants past. Each issue will contain three action-packed adventures of Logan in the format color of black, white, and bloody red! Marvel Comics has brought top-notch artist for this one so I'm going to buy the first issue to check it out. Honestly, Wolverine adventures are usually some of the best of stories in Marvel Comics so I feel it won't dissappoint.
